E-commerce Sales Analysis-Power BI & Python Project

Project Overview
This project analyzes e-commerce performance using Power BI, Python, and SQL. The objective is to evaluate sales growth, profitability, customer behavior, payment trends, and return patterns to generate business insights and recommendations.

Objective
Analyze revenue and profit performance Identify top-performing cities and product categories Understand target customer segments Evaluate return rates and operational issues Study the impact of discounts on profitability

Tools Used
Power BI Python for data cleaning and analysis SQL for data extraction and transformation

Key KPIs
Total Revenue Total Profit Total Orders Average Order Value Return Rate

Page 2 – Product and Operations Analysis

High-margin products Discount versus profit analysis Payment method distribution Return rate by category

Key Insights Performance
Delhi is the leading revenue-generating city. Electronics is the highest-selling category. Maximum revenue comes from the 20 to 30 age group.

Profitability

Samsung S23 and iPhone 14 are the highest-margin products. Profit declines significantly when discounts exceed 10 percent.

Payment Trends

UPI and Credit Cards contribute more than 60 percent of total payments. Cash on Delivery accounts for only 7.7 percent of transactions.

Critical Issues

Appliances category shows a 100 percent return rate and requires immediate investigation. Overall sales and order volume have declined since January.

Recommendations
Investigate the Appliances category for product or supplier issues. Limit discounts beyond 10 percent to protect profit margins. Focus marketing campaigns on the 20 to 30 age segment. Strengthen sales strategy to address the post-January decline. Promote digital payments further due to strong UPI and card adoption.
